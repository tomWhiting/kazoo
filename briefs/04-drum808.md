# Drum808 — TR-808 Drum Machine

## What It Is

A synthesized drum machine inspired by the Roland TR-808. No samples — every sound is generated from oscillators, noise, and envelopes. Pure tones. The 808 kick that rattles car windows. The snappy snare. The sizzling hi-hats.

## What It Does

### Voices

Each voice is a small synth patch. All parameters are tweakable.

| Voice | Synthesis | Key Parameters |
|-------|-----------|----------------|
| **Kick** | Sine oscillator with pitch envelope (starts high, sweeps down fast). Long decay. | pitch, decay, sweep_time, drive |
| **Snare** | Two components: body (triangle osc + pitch envelope) + noise (filtered white noise, band-pass). Mixed. | tone, snappy, decay, noise_level |
| **Closed Hi-hat** | Band-passed noise through high-pass filter. Very short decay. | tone, decay |
| **Open Hi-hat** | Same as closed but longer decay. Closed hat chokes open hat. | tone, decay |
| **Clap** | Filtered noise with multi-trigger envelope (rapid re-triggers for the "clap" texture). | tone, decay |
| **Tom (Low/Mid/High)** | Sine + pitch envelope, similar to kick but shorter and higher. | pitch, decay |
| **Rimshot** | Short noise burst + pitched click. | tone, decay |
| **Cowbell** | Two detuned square oscillators through band-pass filter. | tone, decay |
| **Cymbal** | Metallic noise (multiple detuned square oscillators summed, filtered). Long decay. | tone, decay |

### Sequencer

Built-in 16-step sequencer. Each voice has its own pattern. Supports:

- **Accent** — Per-step velocity emphasis.
- **Pattern chaining** — Chain multiple 16-step patterns into longer sequences.
- **Per-step probability** — 0-100% chance of triggering (fills, variation).
- **Swing** — From Pulse clock or local override.

## Dependencies

- `dsp` crate (oscillators, envelopes, filters, noise, waveshaper)
- `pulse` crate (clock sync)
- `serde` + `serde_json`
- `hound` (WAV output, via dsp)

## Testing

- Render each voice in isolation, verify non-silent and correct duration.
- Kick: verify dominant frequency near specified pitch via FFT.
- Closed hi-hat chokes open hi-hat: render both, verify open hat cuts when closed triggers.
- Pattern accuracy: render a 4/4 kick pattern at 120 BPM, verify kick hits at exactly beat positions.
- Full beat: render a classic 808 pattern, output WAV for listening.
- Accent: compare accented vs non-accented hits, verify amplitude difference.
